About Us

We are an organization focused on the final frontier. We offer weekly updates on all things space. We keep track of current projects from around the world and provide details on those projects so more people can understand what is happening in the space world. We have a comprehensive list of all past and current astronauts. We also have a database full of images captured in space or by other astrological organizations such as NASA.
